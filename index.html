Bio

I am a research scientist in Database and Storage Lab of DAMO Academy in Alibaba Group where I focus on the implementation and optimization of query execution engines on emerging hardware architectures. Before that, I worked as a researcher (PI: Prof. Marianne Winslett) in Advanced Digital Sciences Center (ADSC), a Singapore-based research center established by University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) I obtained my bachelor degree from East China University of Science and Technology and the Ph.D. degree (supervisor: Dr. He Bingsheng) from Nanyang Technological University in 2011 and 2016, respectively.
